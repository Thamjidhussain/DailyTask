Early Life

Pawan Kalyan was born into the famous Konidela family. He is the younger brother of legendary Telugu actor Chiranjeevi. Pawan Kalyan entered the Telugu film industry in 1996 and later became one of the most popular actors in Tollywood. Apart from acting, he is also active in politics and social service.
